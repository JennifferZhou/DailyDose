Welcome to DailyDose

DailyDose is your personal medication scheduler. It helps you organize when to take your medications and supplements throughout the day while automatically detecting and preventing potentially harmful drug interactions.

The system spaces out medications that shouldnâ€™t be taken close together, respects your mealtimes, and lets you export your schedule in a calendar format so you can add it to your calendar app, or download it as a PNG or PDF.

How to Use (6 Simple Steps)

  1. Set Your Health Profile: Check any medical conditions you have and set your meal times (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  2. Add Medications: Enter medication name, dosage, how many times per day, and when to take it (with meal, empty stomach, or anytime)
  3. Review Your List: Check all medications in your list before proceeding
  4. Generate Schedule: Click the green "Generate Schedule" button to create your optimal timing. The system will automatically space out any drugs that interact with each other
  5. Review Safety Alerts: Check the red warning banners to understand any interactions between your medications
  6. Export Your Schedule: Download as PDF to print, or export to sync with Google Calendar or other calendar apps
Important: DailyDose is a scheduling tool, not a medical advisor. Always consult your doctor before starting new medications or changing your routine.
